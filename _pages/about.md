## About

I am a political theorist working on citizenship, legitimacy, justice, and political belonging. My research connects normative political theory with legal and institutional analysis.

I use computational methods (NLP, semantic analysis, hierarchical clustering, and longitudinal frame tracking) as interpretive tools to study political and legal discourse.
